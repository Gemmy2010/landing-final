BikeTrace is a service provider making bicycles smarter and more digitally connected. Our vision is to create an ecosystem of digitally interconnected bicycles, or what we at BikeTrace call "The internet of bicycles".

Our Vision

Often when choosing what clothes to wear we want something that makes us feel good and reflects who we are.This could also apply to what bicycles we chose if we felt confident enough that it would not get stolen.Bike theft is one of the major obstacles to bicycle ownership.
Therefore, our vision is that you should dare to own the bicycle that best suits you. Dare to choose a bike that reflects who you are with the peace of mind that it will not be stolen.

Our Mission

Much like our clothes, bicycles are an extension of ourselves and have the potential to define us. We strive to encourage the widest possible number of individuals to date to own a bicycle that best suits them with the complete confidence that it will not get stolen. We firmly believe that through digitalizing bicycles such level of security can be achieved.
Bicycle theft has, for many years, been the main barriers to ownership.Increased security through digitalization of bikes can equally act as a driver for increase in bicycle ownership.
As we enter a new chapter in human History, there has never been a better time to own a bicycle. This human powered vehicle has the potential of solving both health and sustainability challenges. BikeTrace is pioneering this new bicycle evolution, and it is our mission to develop the conventional bike so it can achieve it's true potential.
